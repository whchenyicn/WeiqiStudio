Capturing happens when a stone or group has no liberties left.

The captured stones are removed from the board.

## Capturing One Stone

To capture one stone, surround all of its liberties with your stones.

When the final liberty is filled, the stone is removed.

## Capturing a Group

Connected stones share liberties.

This means a group may have more liberties than a single stone. To capture the group, you must remove all shared liberties.

## Common Beginner Mistake

Many beginners attack too early without counting liberties.

Before trying to capture, check whether your own stones are also in danger.
