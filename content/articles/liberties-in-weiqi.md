Liberties are the empty intersections directly next to a stone or a group of stones.

A stone needs liberties to stay alive on the board.

## Simple Example

A stone in the middle of the board can have up to four liberties:

- Above
- Below
- Left
- Right

Diagonal points do not count as liberties.

## Why Liberties Matter

When all the liberties of a stone or group are filled by the opponent, that stone or group is captured and removed from the board.

This makes liberties one of the most important ideas in Weiqi.

## Beginner Tip

Before every move, ask yourself:

"How many liberties does this stone or group have?"
