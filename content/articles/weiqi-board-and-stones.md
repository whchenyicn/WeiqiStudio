A Weiqi board is made of horizontal and vertical lines. Stones are placed on the intersections, not inside the squares.

This is one of the first details beginners need to remember.

## Board Sizes

Common board sizes include:

- 9x9 for beginners
- 13x13 for intermediate learning
- 19x19 for standard games

## Black Plays First

In most games, Black plays first.

Because Black has the first move, White often receives compensation points called komi. You do not need to worry too much about komi when you are first learning.

## Stones Do Not Move

Unlike chess pieces, stones do not move after being placed.

Instead, the game develops as both players add more stones to the board.
