Atari means that a stone or group has only one liberty left.

If the opponent fills that final liberty, the stone or group will be captured.

## Atari Is a Warning

When your stones are in atari, you usually need to respond.

You may escape by extending and adding more liberties.

## Atari Is Not Always Good

Beginners often play atari whenever they see it.

But not every atari is useful. Sometimes playing atari only makes your opponent stronger.

## Beginner Tip

Before playing atari, ask:

"What happens after my opponent escapes?"
