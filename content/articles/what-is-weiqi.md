Weiqi is a board game played by two players. One player uses black stones, and the other player uses white stones.

The goal is to control more territory than your opponent.

Even though the rules are simple, the game can become very deep. This is why Weiqi is often described as easy to learn but difficult to master.

## What You Need

To play Weiqi, you need:

- A board
- Black stones
- White stones
- Two players

Beginners often start on a 9x9 board instead of the full 19x19 board.

## The Basic Idea

Players take turns placing one stone at a time on the intersections of the board.

Once a stone is placed, it usually does not move.

The board slowly fills up, and both players try to surround empty spaces and capture weak stones.

## Why Beginners Should Start Small

A 19x19 board can feel overwhelming at first. A 9x9 board teaches the same basic ideas in a much shorter game.

Start small, understand the rules, and then move to larger boards later.
